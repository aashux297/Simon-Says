About Simon Says

Welcome to Simon Says, a fun and engaging memory game designed to test your concentration and Cognitive skills. Inspired by the classic Simon game, our goal is to bring the joy of this timeless challenge to a modern audience.

Simon Says is where entertainment meets Skill-Building! Whether you’re a child or an adult, Simon Says is your go-to game for improving memory, having fun, and challenging your friends.

A world of fun, focus, and Memory mastery! We’ve reinvented the classic Simon game to create a modern, interactive experience for players of all ages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform that not only entertains but also helps players improve their memory, focus, and pattern recognition skills.

We strive to make every moment of gameplay Rewarding, ensuring every player leaves with a smile and a sharper mind.

Is to create a fun and Engaging platform that challenges the mind and fosters focus and concentration. By reimagining a classic game with a modern twist, we strive to inspire people to push their memory and Concentration limits while enjoying a colorful and interactive experience.

Features of Our Game

Interactive Gameplay: Enjoy a sleek and modern design with smooth controls and vibrant animations.

High Score Tracker: Compete with yourself and strive to break your personal best.

Game History: Review your last 5 game scores to track your progress and improvement.

Accessibility: Play Simon Says on any device, anywhere, and anytime.

Dynamic Themes: Choose from a variety of colorful themes to personalize your game experience.

Pause & Resume Feature: Pause the game anytime and pick up exactly where you left off.
